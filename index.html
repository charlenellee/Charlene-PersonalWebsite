Visual Identity Design

Brand Strategy
Graphic Design

This project explores the creation of a visual identity for a personal brand, including a logo, an accompanying poster, and mockups applying the logo to real-world objects.

The design revolves around the themes of authenticity and spontaneity and is represented through two key symbols: a bird and a book. The logo features a mockingbird composed of two elements: the wings, formed by a book with layered pages, and the body, represented by a cursive “s.” The top of the “s” forms the bird’s head while the curves mimic the body and stomach, and the tail symbolizes both a feathered tail and a bookmark. All components work together to express the concept of knowledge, freedom, and expression.

Hack404 Project

UX/UI
Brand Strategy

Hack404 is a 36-hour Toronto-based high school and post-secondary hackathon. For this event, I had the incredible opportunity to collaborate closely with the design team as a Design Organizer to bring Hack404's creative vision to life.

My journey with Hack404 started from designing the first iterations of the website and Instagram posts. Then in the blink of an eye, I was designing some last-minute signage for the venue! Throughout this time, I was able to see everything develop from the ground up and take part in creating a variety of design assets. I even took on design tasks I hadn't tried before like designing a sponsorship package! It truly was a dream working with such talented people as we all came together to tackle different aspects of Hack404's visuals.
